User Guide & Documentation

Welcome to the Topological CDSS Platform. This system translates ancient Traditional Medicine knowledge into modern mathematical algorithms.

1

Select Your Herbs

Search the Pharmacopoeia database on the left. Click on herbs to add them to the central "Digital Prescription Bowl". A minimum of 3 herbs is required.

2

Execute Topological Analysis

Click "Analyze Topological Synergy". The AI converts the properties (Nature, Flavor, Meridians) into binary vectors, plots them in a continuous Euclidean space, and uses Persistent Homology to find geometric structures.

3

Read the Audience-Specific Output

The system will generate tailored advice:

  • Doctors: Review Betti-1 ($H_1$) loops indicating multi-target pathway synergy.
  • Nurses: Review practical care, administration, and monitoring guidelines.
  • Patients: Read simple explanations of how the prescription works for your body.
